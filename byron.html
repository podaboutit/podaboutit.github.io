Lord Byron Mullens

A tribute page to the greatest Bobcat of all time

Here's a timeline of Lord Byron's Bobcats-greatness

  • February 14th, 1989 - Lord Byron is born
  • December 19th, 2011 - Mullens is traded to the Bobcats for a 2013 2nd round pick.
  • December 26th, 2011 - Mullens sets a new career high (10) in points in his 1st game as a Bobcat.
  • December 3rd, 2012 - Byron dunks on LaMarcus Aldridge (pictured above) sending literal shockwaves throughout the league.
  • July 22, 2013 - Having accomplished everything there was to accomplish in a Bobcats uniform. He leaves the team and signs with the L.A. Clippers.
