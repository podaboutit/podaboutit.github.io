Superman

Internet said Superman was dead

but maybe he’s on a break

or packed up and went home.

Now it’s just us.



I remember his cape

every way that it moved

when he passed by us passersby

faster than we could think.



Maybe this will be the last day.

Maybe this is what we’re left with,

his villains and our hands.

Superman died today.
