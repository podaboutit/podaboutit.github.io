A Cosmic Accounting

a thought by Shepard Sullivan as he explains to himself why he’s only a drug store pharmacist
Whatever “it” is…
…it’s not for me.
How in control,
can one truly be?
I was made,
but am I free?
Freedom has a cost, you see.
The books must be balanced.
Dreams must be quashed,
and all that I have gained,
must also be lost.
